# Discussion of features related to design of the app's Database for App Name Verball

## App Goals for users in declining order

1) Learn Verb form memorization in languages with multiplicity of simple verb forms, e.g. Portuguese not English. 
2) Learn Syntax for proper use of forms
3) Additional Vocabulary solely to mix up examples in support of 1 and 2 - not a dictionary

## Data for each language to support learning and puzzle/game approach

1) For each learning language supported (Brazilian Portuguese initially)
Learning language vs translation language (learners native/reference language)
one learning language with match to a translation language pair (one way, bidirectional for what purpose), but expandable - maybe one at a time - Port to Eng or French to Spanish

2) Cross references/categories (verb irregularity, commonly used/frequency:, other forms for comparison/confusion (da/dais, vais/ides), verbs that should be learned in sets (dizer/fazer/trazer, ver & vir, Pôr & Poder, etc.)

3) Puzzle related attribute data like the length of the word, maybe a collection of positional information like where in fazia are the unique f,z,i the a's and thus when comparing to another word, where the crossword overlap would be. Perhaps enabling a search of what word pairs of correct length match at this overlapping position. 


## Analytics
  -Ability to report on encounters with items with characteristics including cross ref/categories above 
  -User encounters (number times seen, last time seen, success/partial success, etc)
  -Eventual FSRS or maybe simpler rankings.
